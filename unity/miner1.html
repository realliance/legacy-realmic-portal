Miner1

Miner1 was a solo development project by Chris that began around 2013. Based off an old Roblox game where the cave generation was on the spot as you mined, he aimed to create a variation in Unity. As the game is singleplayer, it is playable in its current state. The game is equipped with many upgrades for mining, use the shop and mdeltery to reach better items.

Controls
WASD-Move
Hold E-Resource Inventory
Hold Left Click-Miner
Hold B-Recall to top
